About This Project







This Quiz App is a responsive and interactive platform designed for BCA students and learners to test their knowledge in programming subjects like C, C++, Python, HTML, and more. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it includes subject-wise quizzes with difficulty levels, scoring systems, a timer, and user feedback popups. Our goal is to make learning engaging and accessible to all.
